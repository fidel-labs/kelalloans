What do we do?

We're an established, reliable, well-networked credit facilitators based in Addis Ababa. We help facilitate obtaining loans for the diaspora from Ethiopian banks using technology.

You tell us about you, and we do all the legwork of speaking to prominent banks in Ethiopia to assess your profile's eligibility to obtain a loan.
